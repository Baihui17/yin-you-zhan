Tile Jumper 3D

An addictive rhythm-based adventure where every hop is a beat.

About the Game

Tile Jumper 3D is an electrifying and fast-paced arcade game that will put your reflexes and timing to the ultimate test. The premise is simple yet captivating: control a bouncing ball and guide it across an endless, winding path of tiles suspended in a vibrant 3D world. Your goal is to jump for as long as possible without falling into the abyss.

As you progress, the path becomes more challenging, the speed increases, and the tiles require more precise jumps. Paired with an energetic soundtrack, each successful hop feels like a satisfying beat drop, creating an immersive and hypnotic gameplay experience. Are you ready to find your flow and set a new high score?

How to Play

Controls

Simply use your Mouse. Press and hold the left mouse button, then drag left or right to guide the ball onto the next tile. Release to land the jump.

Objective

Your mission is to survive for as long as possible. Jump from tile to tile, collect gems, and avoid falling off the path. The further you go, the higher your score!
